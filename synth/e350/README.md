Synthtec E350 Morphing Terrarium



# E350


## Introduction


The E350 Morphing Terrarium is a Euro-rack voltage controlled wavetable oscillator
with 3 banks of 64 waves, two outputs and a unique 2-D 'terrain morphing' method
of interpolation.
